Description

Herbaceous plant about 10-30 cm high, the stem is simple or branched, upright, cylindrical, yellowish-green. Leaves are alternately lanceolate-ovate, end rounded to ovate, 0.5-2.5 cm long.

Distribution

Kingdom of Saudi Arabia - Kuwait - Sultanate of Oman - Yemen and in general in the Arabian Peninsula, and in Tunisia and Morocco, Algeria, Egypt, Libya, Iraq, Lebanon and Syria.

Environment and Reproduction

It spreads on sandy soils.

Use and toxicity

The lactic sap contains selenium and ivoryon that causes irritation to all animals, diarrhea, swollen eyes and mouth, and severe heartburn in the mouth and throat.
