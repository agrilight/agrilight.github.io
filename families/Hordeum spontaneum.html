Common names

wild barley, spontaneous barley

Description

Wild barley is an annual grass and is very similar in form to cultivated barley but has slightly narrower leaves, longer stems

Distribution

North Africa, the Middle East, parts of the Indian subcontinent and south-west China. Its range includes Libya, Egypt, Palestine, Lebanon, Jordan, Syria, Crete, Cyprus, Turkey, Iran, Iraq, Afghanistan, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Kyrgyzstan, Turkmenistan, Uzbekistan, Pakistan, India and the Sichuan and Xizang provinces of China
