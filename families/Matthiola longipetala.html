Common names

Pulsatilla Pulgaris, wild anthurium.

Description

An annual herb, fluffy, and may have scattered glandular streaks, their length ranges between 10-40 cm. The stems are branched from the base, few leaves, the leaves are simple, mostly found in the lower part of the plant, linear to shaft or oblong in shape,

Geographical distribution

It spreads in the eastern Mediterranean and western Iranian Turanian regions.

Environment and Reproduction

One of the plants of the beautiful season, we find it in steppe and plateau environments with dry and semi-arid climates. It propagates by seeds.

Pastoral importance

Animals are well cared for, especially in the vegetative phase and before flowering.
