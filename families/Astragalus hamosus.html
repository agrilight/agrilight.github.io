Common names

Antlers, Hookworms.

Discription

species spreads in the Arabian Peninsula, the Levant, iraq and North African countries.

Environment and Reproduction

One of the beautiful season plants, it is spread from sea level to a height of 100 m above sea level, where it finances the areas of the steppes, plains, streams and depressions inhabited by rainwater for limited periods. Reproduces with seeds that are planted in the soil at the beginning of winter

Pastoral importance

The plant is well cared for by animals, and is considered one of the climax annual components of plant communities.
